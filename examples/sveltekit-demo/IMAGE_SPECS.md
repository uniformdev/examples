# Cherry Product Image Specifications

## General Guidelines

### Style Requirements
- **Aesthetic:** Premium, minimal, futuristic Apple-inspired
- **Background:** Clean white/light gray gradient OR transparent for flexibility
- **Lighting:** Soft studio lighting with subtle cherry red rim/accent lights
- **Perspective:** 3/4 angle, slightly elevated view
- **Mood:** Aspirational, premium, sci-fi elegance

### Technical Specs
- **Aspect Ratio:** 1:1 (square) for product cards, 16:9 for heroes
- **Resolution:** Minimum 2048x2048px for products, 3840x2160px for heroes
- **Format:** JPG or PNG (transparent if needed)
- **Style:** Photorealistic product render, NOT illustration

### Brand Colors for Accent Lighting
- Cherry Red: `#E62E5C`
- Cherry Light: `#E8708A`
- Subtle Magenta: `#E626A8`
- Teal accent (sparingly): `#29A3A3`

```
background, text, watermark, logo, busy patterns, cartoonish, illustration 
style, oversaturated, neon colors, gaming aesthetic, aggressive design, 
sharp angles, visible wires, complex machinery, steampunk, cyberpunk grunge
```

## Post-Processing Notes

1. **Color grading:** Ensure cherry red accents match `#E62E5C`
2. **Background:** Should fade to pure white at edges for easy integration
3. **Shadows:** Soft, diffused contact shadows only
4. **Contrast:** High enough to pop but not harsh
5. **Consistency:** All products should feel like part of the same family
